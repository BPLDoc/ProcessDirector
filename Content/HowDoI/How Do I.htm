How Do I...?

This page presents links to software simulations, videos and/or text walk-throughs for performing various tasks in Process Director. This page is a work in progress and will grow over time, so be sure to check back here for new content.

Some of the presentations linked from this page have been suggested by Process Director users and customers. If you have a suggestion, please use the Feedback Form at the bottom of the page to submit your suggestion for How Do I? topics.

Software Simulations

This section is designed to provide a series of links to software simulations to walk you through the process of performing different tasks in Process Director. These simulations replicate the look and feel of Process Director in your browser, and walk you through the process of performing the task as if you were doing to in the product. Button clicks, responses, and configurations are all performed via these simulations, which also provide guidance and text commentary as you work through them.

Each of the links below will open the simulations in a new browser window. To start each simulation, simply click the play button that will appear on the screen when the simulation loads.
