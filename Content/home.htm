BP Logix Product Documentation

Click here to see how the Process Director documentation system works, how to search for information, and how to filter search results.

The BP Logix icon on the lower right corner of each page will, when clicked, open the AI chat box to answer your questions about Process Director. You can also use the Feedback button at the bottom of each page to ask a quick question or provide feedback about the documentation.

To get started quickly, you can go directly to the Quick Links Page or use the Quick Links menu at the top of the page to see a collection of links to the most often-used features of Process Director.

The documentation for Process Director is largely reference documentation. For training on how to use the product, please contact your sales representative for a login code to our Online Training Portal. The training portal contains several self-paced training courses that cover all aspects of using Process Director to create low-code/no-code applications. A link to the training portal is also available in the main menu at the top of each page.

Live Product Webinars

BP Logix offers two recurring webinars. Registering for the webinars will enable you to attend, and ensure that you receive notification reminders prior to each webinar.

Training Webinar

Held every Thursday from 10AM Pacific (1PM Eastern) until 12PM Pacific (3PM Eastern). Each week, a specific process Director topic will be taught live during the webinar. You can see a schedule of Training Webinar topics at the BP Logix Support site. Click here to register for the Training Webinar.

Feature Spotlight Webinar

Held on the second Tuesday of every month, and runs from 10AM Pacific (1PM Eastern) until 10:30AM Pacific (1:30PM Eastern). Each month, the webinar will provide updates on new or upcoming features in Process Director.Click here to register for the Feature Spotlight Webinar.
