Lesson 2 Exercise: Creating a Process Timeline

Practice Exercise Create a Process Timeline

Instructions

Create a Process Timeline by following the directions below.

1. In the Student Exercise Folder, create a new Process Timeline named "Change of Student Major".

2. Create a new Timeline Activity.

3. Save and close the new Process Timeline.

4. Open the Change of Major Request form we created earlier.

5. Connect the Process Timeline to the form.

6. Run and submit the form to ensure the Form starts running the Process Timeline.

7. Click the Complete button at the bottom of the form to complete the Activity and end the running Process Timeline.

8. Open the Process Timeline, and, in the Options section, connect the form to the timeline.
