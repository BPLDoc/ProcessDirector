Lesson 1 Exercise: Creating a Business Rule

Practice Exercise Create and Configure a Knowledge View

Instructions

Create and configure a Knowledge View:

  1. Create a new Knowledge View named "Active Change of Major Request".
  2. On the Properties Tab of the Knowledge View, associate the Change of Major Request form with the Knowledge View.
  3. On the Options tab, configure the Knowledge View to return only Form instances.
  4. Run the Knowledge View to test whether you are seeing the expected data.
