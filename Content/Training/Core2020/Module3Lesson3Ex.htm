Lesson 3 Exercise: Applying Business Rules to a Form

Practice Exercise Configure the Form to show or hide embedded sections based on the value of a Business Rule.

Instructions

Configure the Change of Major Request form as follows:

  1. Following the instructions in this lesson, add an Embedded Section control to include the Department Approval heading and table on the form.
    1. Name the Section DepartmentSection.

  2. add an Embedded Section control to include the Dean's Office Approval heading and table on the form.

    1. Name the Section DeanSection.
  3. On the Form Controls tab of the Form definition, make the following configuration changes:
    1. Set the DepartmentSection as Field is Visible When the "Department Approval Required" Business Rule returns "True", and Otherwise Hidden.
    2. Set the DeanSection as Field is Visible When the "More Than 150SH?" Business Rule returns "True", and Otherwise Hidden.
  4. Run and submit the form to test whether the sections are hidden as expected.
