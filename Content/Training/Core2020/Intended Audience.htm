Intended Audience

This course is intended for users who will design and create Forms, Process Timelines, Business Rules and Knowledge Views in Process Director. The course objectives are designed for anyone with a need to learn the basic concepts behind Process Director objects and the skills required to implement process automation using Process Director. This includes:

  • Persons who will design and/or create Process Director objects to model a process.
  • Persons who will administer Process Director installations.
  • Persons who will directly supervise Process Director designers and administrators.

This course assumes that students are already familiar with the basic concepts of Business Process Management (BPM). The course concentrates on the application of pre-existing BPM knowledge to the specific functionality of Process Director.
