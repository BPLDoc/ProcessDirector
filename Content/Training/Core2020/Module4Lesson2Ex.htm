Lesson 2 Exercise: Knowledge View Columns and Filters

Practice Exercise Configure a Knowledge View

Instructions

Configure a Knowledge View.

  1. Configure the Columns tab of the Active Change of Major Requests Knowledge View to display the following columns:
    1. Name: Make no changes to this column.
    2. Student: StudentName form field.
    3. Desired Major: DesiredMajor form field.
    4. Department Approval: DepartmentApprovalRequired form field.
    5. Semester Hours: form field.
  2. Configure the Filter tab of the Active Change of Major Requests Knowledge View to use the condition "Timeline Status = Active".
  3. Update and Run the Knowledge View to ensure the expected data is displayed.
