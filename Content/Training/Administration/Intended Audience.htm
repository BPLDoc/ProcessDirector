Intended Audience

This course is intended for users who will perform administrative tasks in Process Director. The course objectives are designed for anyone with a need to learn the basic concepts behind Process Director administration and the skills required to configure Process Director settings, users, and workspaces. This includes:

  • Persons who will configure and troubleshoot Process Director installations.
  • Persons who will administer Process Director installations.
  • Persons who will directly supervise Process Director administrators.

This course assumes that students are already familiar with the basic concepts of Windows Server administration and have a general familiarity with the basic functions and operations of Process Director. The course concentrates on the application of pre-existing System Admininstrator knowledge to the specific functionality of Process Director.
